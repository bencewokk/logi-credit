Overview

This is a small starter site for the Logi Credit project. Each team member has a page with their responsibilities and a place to add notes or prototype UI.

Quick start

  1. Open `website/index.html` in your browser.
  2. Edit your person page under `website/people/`.
  3. Use `styles.css` and `app.js` for shared UI and interactivity.
